About

Unlim8ted Studio Productions

We build films, games, music, books, software, and AI products—crafted as cinematic experiences with no creative limits.

Contact See work What we make
Mission

Boundless creativity, shipped

Unlim8ted Studio Productions is a U.S.-based entertainment studio founded in 2019. We explore storytelling across mediums— blending procedural ideas, interactive design, and experimentation with emerging tech.

Films

Stories that feel real—even when they aren’t

  • The Life of a Meatball An animated adventure exploring identity and belonging through humor and heart.
  • The Glitch An experimental short film about reality, forgiveness, and the imperfections that make us human.

We treat film like a playground for mood, mystery, and big emotional swings—paired with bold visuals.

Games

Retro DNA with modern ambition

  • Square Pixels A 2D sandbox adventure with exploration, crafting, and chaotic pixel-world storytelling.
  • Unicornia A speed-running platformer built for precision, flow, and replayable challenge.

Our games lean community-driven, systems-heavy, and deeply replayable—classic feel, new twists.

Books

Worlds you can live inside

  • The Glitch (Book) A deeper expansion of the short film’s themes—imperfection, meaning, and choice.
  • Alien and The Crystal’s Conflict Sci-fi / fantasy conflict, artifacts, and the kind of mystery that keeps escalating.
  • The Hidden Magic School & Stranded Ancient Fantasy stories centered on discovery, survival, and ancient power systems.
Audio

Music and podcasts

We release music across genres and experiment with podcast formats—informational, fictional, and everything in between.
