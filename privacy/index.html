Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Unlim8ted Studio Productions collects, uses, and protects information across our website, games, software, and other digital services.

Last updated: Contact: support@unlim8ted.com
Quick summary: We collect only what we need to run our services, improve them, and keep them secure.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information: If you create an account or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and other contact details you provide.
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, time spent, interactions, and basic feature usage to understand what’s working.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, approximate location (from IP), and device identifiers for security and diagnostics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: Used to support functionality, remember preferences, and measure performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve services: Operate features, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Personalization: Remember settings and preferences where available.
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and send important service updates.
  • Analytics: Measure engagement and improve the experience.
  • Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, and secure accounts.

How We Protect Your Data

  • Encryption: We use secure connections (HTTPS) and industry-standard protections where applicable.
  • Access controls: Access to sensitive data is limited to authorized personnel and service providers.
  • Monitoring: We monitor for suspicious activity to protect accounts and systems.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers: Vendors that help us operate services (e.g., hosting, analytics, payments) under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or valid legal process.
  • With consent: If you explicitly authorize sharing for a specific purpose.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update: You may request access to or correction of your information.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of promotional messages (if any) via unsubscribe links or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations and legitimate business needs.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to support functionality and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided information, contact us and we’ll work to delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates are effective when posted. If changes are significant, we may provide an additional notice on the website.
